Lesson 1:

Objective

Congratulations! You have decided to take your first steps to becoming a wizard! Our first lesson is to understand the objective of a duel, to drain your opponent of life points. In most duels your opponent will start with 20 life points. To drain them you need to find a way to do damage to them or to force them into losing life. Damage can be dealt by hitting them with creatures or some direct spells. In a future lesson we will look at combat and hitting your opponent with a creature, but for now just remember that you have to defeat your opponent before they defeat you.

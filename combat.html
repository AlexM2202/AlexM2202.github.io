Lesson 4:

Combat

Lesson 4 is combat. Here you will take your first steps towards victory. We will start with the basics of attack, power and toughness. Use the card at the bottom again for a reference. Our angel has a power and toughness of 4/4. The first number is power or damage, and the second is toughness or health. This means if this creature hits a player it does 4 damage. A player can choose to block. In the case of our angel they would need a flying creature or a creature with reach because those are the only ones that can block a flying creature. If a creature blocks our angel and they have under 4 toughness they would die, but if they have over 4 toughness they would live and then get healed at the end of the turn. Most creatures would need to be tapped to attack. Or angel is an exception because it has the keyword vigilance. Be careful because if your creature is tapped you can not use it to block. Also, if you played the creature this turn it can not attack because of “summoning sickness” so wait until next turn to use it.

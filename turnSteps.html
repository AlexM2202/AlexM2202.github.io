Lesson 3:

Turn Phases

Welcome to lesson 3. Here we will dive into the steps that make up your turn. Again there is a picture to reference below. First we have upkeep. This is where we untap all cards we have tapped. After, we will check if any cards we have on the battlefield have abilities that trigger during our upkeep. Next we draw our card for the turn. Now we enter our main phase. Here we can cast spells and play our one land per turn. After you are satisfied with your plays we can move to combat. This is your chance to swing at your opponent and do some damage. We will cover the details of combat next lesson because it can get complicated. After combat we enter our post combat main phase which is exactly like our first main phase. Last we enter our end step and cleanup. Here we heal our creatures and discard down to hand size.
